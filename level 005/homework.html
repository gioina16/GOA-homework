shrek

Shrek is a fictional ogre, the protagonist of the Shrek franchise. He is initially portrayed as a solitary creature who lives in a swamp, but he embarks on an adventure to save Princess Fiona and becomes her husband, as well as the father of their children. The character was created by William Steig in his 1990 picture book Shrek!

shreks dragon

The dragon from Shrek is a large, fire-breathing, female dragon who is initially introduced as the fearsome guardian of Princess Fiona's tower. She is later revealed to be Donkey's romantic interest and eventually his wife, with whom he has a family of hybrid children called Dronkeys. Despite her intimidating appearance and powerful abilities, the dragon is shown to have a caring and protective nature, especially towards Donkey and her children.
